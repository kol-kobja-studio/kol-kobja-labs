About Kol-Kobja Labs

Kol-Kobja Labs is an independent R&D initiative that blends hardware engineering, embedded software, and machine learning to build practical prototypes and products.

Mission

Translate research and ideas into working prototypes that solve real problems.

Approach

Rapid prototyping, iterative testing, and close integration of hardware and software components.

Embedded Systems

Firmware, RTOS, peripheral drivers and low-level optimization.

IoT & Cloud

Secure connectivity, MQTT/HTTP pipelines and cloud integrations.

ML at the Edge

Lightweight ML models, model optimization and sensor fusion.
